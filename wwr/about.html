Our mission is to bring the thrill of the river to adventurers of all ages. Whether you’re seeking a serene float or an adrenaline-pumping ride, we promise an unforgettable experience.

History

Founded in 1998 by river-running enthusiasts, our company began with a single raft and a dream. Over the years we’ve grown into a trusted outfitter, guiding thousands of guests safely down some of the most exciting rivers in the region.
